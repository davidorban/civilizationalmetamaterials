# Collaboration on the Trial

The paper proposes a 12-week stepped-wedge cluster-randomized trial in government grant review
panels. The protocol, statistical analysis plan, and instruments are in `experiments/` and are
explicitly marked **"proposed, not yet registered"** — no IRB application has been filed.

## Institutions we are looking for

* Government or foundation grant review bodies with ≥ 6 panels per review cycle
* Research institutions studying deliberative processes, AI governance, or decision science
* Organizations able to support IRB approval and informed-consent workflows

## What we provide

* Full protocol draft (`experiments/protocol/protocol-v0.1.md`)
* Statistical analysis plan and pre-specified sensitivity analyses (`experiments/protocol/SAP.md`)
* OSF preregistration template, ready to paste (`experiments/protocol/preregistration-OSF.md`)
* Provenance-class rubric for reviewers (`experiments/instruments/provenance-checklist.pdf`)
* Verification timer app for τ measurement (`experiments/instruments/verification-timer-app/`)
* Synthetic data generator and analysis scripts for end-to-end validation
* Analysis code that can be swapped to real panel data
